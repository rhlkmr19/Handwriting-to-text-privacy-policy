1. Introduction

AiTricker ("we", "us", "our") operates the Handwriting to Text mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

By installing or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Anonymous Device Identifier

When you first open the App, a unique anonymous identifier is generated using Firebase Authentication (anonymous sign-in). This identifier is not linked to your name, email, phone number, or any personal account. It is used solely for managing your scan quota and verifying purchases.

2.2 Camera Images (Temporary Processing Only)

When you scan handwritten text, the captured or gallery-selected image is compressed and sent to our server only for text recognition (OCR) processing.

Important: Images are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers. Once the recognized text is returned to your device, the image is immediately discarded from our server. We do not retain, review, or use your images for any other purpose.

2.3 Language Preference

Your selected OCR language (e.g., English, Hindi, French) is sent along with the image to select the appropriate text recognition model. This is not stored permanently on our server.

2.4 Purchase Verification

If you upgrade to Premium or Pro, your Google Play purchase token is sent to our server to verify the purchase. We verify the token with Google and store your upgraded tier status (linked to your anonymous device ID). We do not receive or store your payment details (credit card, billing address, etc.) — all payment processing is handled by Google Play.

2.5 Usage Counters

We track scan counts, batch scan usage, and PDF export counts associated with your anonymous device ID to enforce free-tier limits. No personal information is linked to these counters.

3. Information Stored Only on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to our servers:

  • Scanned text results, scan history, and folders (local Room database)
  • Image thumbnails of scanned pages (app's internal storage, max 300px, never uploaded)
  • App settings — default language, crop toggle, auto-enhance toggle, AI insights toggle
  • Translated text (ML Kit Translation runs entirely on-device)
  • PDF files you export (stored in app's cache directory)
We have no access to any of your locally stored data. You can delete it at any time using the Trash feature or by uninstalling the App.

4. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not collect contacts or call logs
  • We do not display advertisements or collect advertising IDs
  • We do not use analytics or crash reporting SDKs
  • We do not store your images on our servers
  • We do not access files on your device beyond the images you select for scanning
